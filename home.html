Aimee's Hometown

I was born in Detroit, MI and raised in a suburb called Brownstown. It was an odd arrangment, our township had a Wyandotte city zip code, I attended Woodhaven public schools, and we basically lived in Riverview (based on the boarder lines). In 2013 I moved to Detroit and have been there ever since(though I have lived in a few differnt parts of the city). Currently, I live in a histroic neighborhood called the University District which is full of old character and charm.

Aimee's Education

Growing up, I attended Woodhaven Public Schools, but in high school I switched to Gabriel Richard Catholic School. The school pushed me harder academically and was extremely competitive in sports. While attending I played volleyball, tennis, and softball while taking honors and AP courses. When graduation came, I chose to attend Central Michigan University for it's size, distance from home, and graphic design program. Graphic design did not work out, I ended up majoring in Entrepreneurship, but CMU was the right place for me to thrive academically and socially. When I wasn't studing small business, I was an active member of Sigma Kappa Sorority. I still have those friendships almost 20 years later. As a new grad in 2008, the job market was T-O-U-G-H. I love learning and felt I could use more education while waiting for the job market to open up, so I attended Eastern Michigan University and received a Master's of Science in Apparel, Merchandising, and Textiles. Through this degree, I was also able to travel to Los Angeles, Paris, and London to learn about different fashion markets. That was the highlight of my Master's program!
